This mobile application is part of an immersive, speculative design proposal and research project by Luke Sturgeon.

The Citizen Rotation Office is an uncomfortable fictional service and future scenario, designed as a provocation for in-context debate. Participants discuss their concerns for our cities, homes, communities, and the role of technology and data in the future.
